# Talaria Model Comparison Guide

Choosing the right Talaria e-bike depends on your riding style, experience level, and intended use. Here's a comprehensive comparison of the main Talaria models and what to consider for insurance.

## Talaria Sting

The flagship model that put Talaria on the map.

### Key Specifications
- **Motor**: 6kW peak power (4kW nominal)
- **Top Speed**: 50-60 mph (depending on configuration)
- **Battery**: 60V 38Ah stock (upgradable)
- **Weight**: ~115 lbs
- **Range**: 40-60 miles depending on terrain

### Best For
- Trail riding enthusiasts
- Weekend warriors
- First-time high-performance e-bike owners
- Those wanting a balance of power and control

### Insurance Value Range
Typical insured values: **$4,500 - $7,000** (including common modifications)

---

## Talaria Sting R

The race-ready evolution of the Sting.

### Key Specifications
- **Motor**: 8kW peak power
- **Top Speed**: 60+ mph
- **Battery**: 60V 45Ah stock
- **Suspension**: Upgraded DNM/FastAce
- **Weight**: ~120 lbs

### Key Upgrades Over Sting
- Higher power motor controller
- Improved suspension with more travel
- Better brakes (often hydraulic upgrade)
- Reinforced frame components

### Best For
- Experienced riders
- Competitive/race use
- Aggressive trail riding
- Those wanting maximum performance

### Insurance Value Range
Typical insured values: **$6,000 - $9,000** (with race modifications)

---

## Talaria MX4

Full-size motocross platform for serious off-road performance.

### Key Specifications
- **Motor**: 10kW peak power
- **Top Speed**: 65+ mph
- **Battery**: 72V 40Ah+
- **Suspension**: Full MX-spec
- **Weight**: ~140 lbs

### Key Features
- Full-size MX frame geometry
- Professional-grade suspension
- High-capacity battery system
- Competition-ready out of the box

### Best For
- MX/enduro enthusiasts
- Professional use
- Riders coming from gas motorcycles
- Maximum power seekers

### Insurance Value Range
Typical insured values: **$8,000 - $14,000**

---

## Talaria XXX

Premium street/trail hybrid with road-legal options.

### Key Specifications
- **Motor**: Varies by configuration
- **Top Speed**: Configurable for road legal use
- **Features**: Lights, signals, mirrors available
- **Build Quality**: Premium components throughout

### Best For
- Dual sport (street + trail) riders
- Urban commuters wanting off-road capability
- Those needing road-legal e-bike
- Premium build quality seekers

### Insurance Value Range
Typical insured values: **$7,000 - $12,000**

## Insurance Considerations by Model

### Theft Risk
All Talaria models are high-theft targets due to:
- High resale value
- Desirable brand recognition
- Portable size
- Easy to sell parts

**Our recommendation**: Comprehensive theft coverage is essential for any Talaria.

### Modification Coverage
Common upgrades that should be covered:

| Modification | Typical Cost | Common On |
|-------------|--------------|-----------|
| 72V Battery Upgrade | $800-1,500 | All models |
| Controller Upgrade | $300-600 | Sting, Sting R |
| Suspension Upgrade | $500-1,200 | Sting, MX4 |
| Brake Upgrade | $200-500 | All models |
| Display/Controller | $150-400 | All models |

### Liability Needs
Higher performance = higher liability risk. We recommend:
- **Sting/XXX**: Minimum $100k liability
- **Sting R/MX4**: Consider $300k+ liability
